JHS Girls Who Code

Who are we?

We are a chapter of a worldwide organization that strives to close the gender gap and raise the next generation of female leaders.

What do we do?

At our meetings, members learn coding, discover STEM activities and internships, attend field-trips, build (outreach) connections across the country, and much more!
